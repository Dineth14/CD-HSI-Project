Week 3 — Controlled Benchmark Design

Planned Work

  • Literature review on Vision encoders and their performance review
  • Observing novel architectures and their impact on semantic segmentation and object detection for change detection
  • Experimentation with different model configurations and architectures

Findings

Observations

  • CNN models are light weight and efficient for training
  • Transformers provide better context understanding but are computationally intensive
  • Hybrid models can leverage the strengths of both CNNs and Transformers for improved change detection performance
  • Novel state space models show potential for better performance and generalization

Decisions

  • Review on how the models understand spatial and temporal features.
  • Experimenting vision encoders with a controlled architecture and training configuration.
