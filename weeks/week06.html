Week 6 — Boundary and Domain-Shift Analysis

Status: Completed · Dates: 31/05/2026 – 06/06/2026
← Week 5  ·  Home  ·  Week 7 →

Planned Work

  • Analysis of boundary detection performance of implemented models.
  • Investigation of domain-shift effects on model performance.
  • Comparison of boundary detection metrics across different datasets.

Findings

Observations

  • We performed controlled benchmarking on both LoveDA and ISPRS Potsdam datasets, evaluating CNN, Transformer, and State-Space Models (SSMs). The complete cross-domain and source-only results are detailed in Table I.
  • We conducted a boundary and interior diagnostic analysis on LoveDA (Table II). A significant drop in performance is observed at boundary regions (Δ) compared to interior regions across all models, highlighting boundary detection as a primary bottleneck.
  • Domain-shift effects are pronounced in cross-domain evaluations (U→R and R→U), indicating limited generalization without targeted domain adaptation.
