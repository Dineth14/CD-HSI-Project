Week 6 — Boundary and Domain-Shift Analysis

Status: In progress · Dates: 31/05/2026 – 06/06/2026
← Week 5  ·  Home  ·  Week 7 →

Planned Work

  • Analysis of boundary detection performance of implemented models.
  • Investigation of domain-shift effects on model performance.
  • Comparison of boundary detection metrics across different datasets.

Findings

Observations

  • We calculated boundary miou for each model and compared it with the interior miou of the same model.
  • Domain-shift effects were observed, indicating that models trained on one dataset may not generalize well to another.

Decisions

  • Literature review on how boundary detection and refinement have been used in the published models and also identifying potential improvements for our approach.
